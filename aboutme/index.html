Hey there, my name is Betty Fan

I am a Virginia Tech senior from the DC area, studying Business Information Technology and minoring in Professional & Technical Writing. If I am not off in the middle of a mountain wandering around the great outdoors, you will most likely find me traveling, splattering paint on a canvas, getting lost in a novel, laughing with friends over a latte in an urban coffee shop, discovering new restaurants, or capturing memories through photographs. I am infatuated with finding beauty everywhere and simple moments. I firmly believe that life is an adventure and entirely what you make of it. Be bold, pack your bags, lace up your shoes, and try something new.

Thanks for stopping by!
